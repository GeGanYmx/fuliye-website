Why starts EXOPS?
Exoskeleton can be regarded as the quite frontier technology, which askes people to invest lots of time and money. However, if there is an existing platform for them to conduct further develop and innovate, it will accelerate the exploration and increase the communication and is enabling new ways for meeting different expectations of individual users.
Who needs EXOPS?
EXOPS is the first of its kind to provide an software-centric platform with modular hardware while delivering the multiple locomotion patterns to global creators. It provides hardware, API, SDK, development support, etc.
